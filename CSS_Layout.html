CUSTOM PATTERNS

due week fourteen

ARTS 105

Photoshop for Non-Majors

I will be giving a demo in class on how to create and use custom patterns.

Your assignment will be to use different patterns to build an image. We will use existing patterns in Photoshop and download some from the internet. I will also show you how to create your own patterns.

We will be using coloring book pages as an image base for this assignment. Go here or here to find pages to color.

Use a layer mask with your patterns to "color" in different elements on your page. Use at least five different patterns within your coloring page. Use additional colors and effects as well, such as adjustment layer tweaks.
